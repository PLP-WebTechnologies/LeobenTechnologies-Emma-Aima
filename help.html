Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?
Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your purchase.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment options listed at checkout.
How can I track my order?
After your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. Use it to track your shipment on the courier's website.
Can I return or exchange a product?
Yes, you can return or exchange products within 14 days of delivery. Please see our Returns Policy for details.
Who do I contact for support?
Contact our support team at support@leobentechnologies.com or use the contact form on our Contact Us page.

Gadget & Electronics Care Advice

  • Keep your devices clean and dry. Use a microfiber cloth for screens.
  • Avoid overcharging batteries to extend their lifespan.
  • Install software updates regularly for security and performance.
  • Use protective cases and screen guards to prevent damage.
  • Back up important data to cloud storage or external drives.
  • Store electronics in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
