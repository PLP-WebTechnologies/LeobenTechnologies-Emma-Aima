Our Story

Welcome to LEOBEN TECHNOLOGIES, your premier destination for cutting-edge technology solutions. Founded in 2023 by visionary entrepreneur Emma Aima Leoben, our company has rapidly grown from a small startup to a recognized leader in the tech industry.

What began as a passion project in a modest home office has blossomed into a thriving enterprise dedicated to delivering innovative, eco-friendly technology products. We're committed to pushing boundaries while maintaining our core values of sustainability, quality, and exceptional customer service.

At LEOBEN TECHNOLOGIES, we believe technology should enhance lives without compromising our planet's future. That's why we've implemented rigorous sustainability standards across our operations, from product design to packaging and shipping.

Augmented Reality Glasses

AR (Augmented Reality) glasses are wearable devices that overlay digital information—such as images, text, or 3D models—onto the real world through transparent lenses. In today's world, AR glasses are used for navigation, real-time translation, hands-free work instructions, immersive gaming, education, remote assistance, and enhancing shopping experiences by blending digital content with the user's physical environment.

Virtual Reality Glasses

VR (Virtual Reality) glasses are wearable devices that immerse users in a fully digital, computer-generated environment, blocking out the real world. They use screens or lenses to display 3D visuals and often include sensors for head tracking. In today's world, VR glasses are used for immersive gaming, virtual tours, training simulations, education, remote collaboration, therapy, and entertainment.

SpaceTop G1 Screenless Laptop

A screenless laptop, such as the SpaceTop G1, is a computing device that replaces the traditional physical display with augmented reality (AR) or virtual reality (VR) technology. Instead of a built-in screen, it projects a virtual workspace into the user's field of view using specialized glasses or headsets.
